Title:Human Resources
Description: Human resource (HR) teams handle a wide range of employee grievances. These grievances can be broadly categorized into the following types:
Pay and benefits,Work conditions,Management practices,Harassment and discrimination,Disciplinary actions,Promotion and recognition,Termination of employment.
HR teams play a crucial role in addressing employee grievances in a fair, impartial, and timely manner.

Title: ICT
Description: ICT teams handle a wide range of grievances related to IT infrastructure, systems, and services. These grievances can be broadly categorized into the following types:
Hardware issues,Software issues,Email issues,Hosting issues,Systems issues,Network issues,General IT support.
ICT teams play a crucial role in ensuring that the organization's IT infrastructure is reliable, secure, and efficient.

Title: Finance
Description: The finance team in a software organization handles a wide range of grievances, including:
Payroll issues,Expense reimbursement issues,Accounts receivable issues,Accounts payable issues,Financial reporting issues,Tax compliance issues
The finance team plays a crucial role in ensuring that the organization's financial records are accurate and compliant with all applicable laws and regulations.

Scheduling meetings,Preparing documents,Providing information about company policies
The Travel, Hospitality, and Admin team plays a crucial role in ensuring that the organization runs smoothly and efficiently.
